About Us

Molecular Processing, LLC is a newly formed chemical distillation company based in Hot Springs, Arkansas. The company is part of the network supporting the Polycoat / American Polymer group of companies, a third-generation family-owned enterprise founded in 1984. With over 1 million square feet of U.S. manufacturing facilities, the group serves diverse industries including construction, roofing, flooring, packaging, infrastructure, and waterproofing. This website serves as a simple, informative platform to introduce our services and capabilities.
